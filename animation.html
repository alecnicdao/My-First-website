My all time starting five

NBA Players

Gary Payton-

Gary Payton is a retired National Basketball Association (NBA) Hall of Fame (HOF) player. He is a 9x All-Star, 1995-96 steals champion, 2005-06 NBA champion, 9x All-Defensive, 9x All-NBA, 1990-91 All-Rookie, and 1995-96 Defensive Player of the Year. The teams Payton played for was the Seattle Supersonics, Milwaukee Bucks, Los Angeles Lakers, Boston Celtics, and then the Miami Heat.

Michael Jordan-

Michael Jordan is a retired National Basketball Association (NBA) Hall of Fame (HOF) player. He is a 14x all-star, 10x scoring champion, 3x steals champion, 6x NBA champion, 11x All-NBA, 9x All-Defensive, 1984-85 All-Rookie, 3x All-Star Most Valuable Player (MVP), 1987-88 Defensive Player of the Year, 6x Finals MVP, 5x MVP, and 1984-85 Rookie of the Year. The teams Jordan player for were the Chicago Bulls and the Washington Wizards.

LeBron James-

LeBron James is a NBA player currently playing for the Los Angeles Lakers. He is a 15x All-Star, 2007-08 scoring champion, 3x NBA champion, 14x All-NBA, 6x All-Defensive, 2003-04 All-Rookie, 3x All-Star MVP, 3x Finals MVP, 4x MVP, 2003-04 Rookie of the Year, and a future Hall of Famer. The teams he has played for are the Cleveland Cavaliers, and Miami Heat.

Karl Malone-

Karl Malone is a retired HOF NBA player that played for the Utah Jazz and Los Angeles Lakers. He is a 14x All-Star, 14x All-NBA, 4x All-Defensive, 1985-86 All-Rookie, 2x All-Star MVP, and 2x MVP.

Shaquille O'Neal-

Shaquille O'Neal is a retired HOF NBA player. He is a 15x All-Star, 2x scoring champ, 4x NBA champ, 14x All-NBA, 3x All-Defensive, 1992-93 All-Rookie, 3x All-Star MVP, 3x Finals MVP, 1999-00 MVP, and 1992-93 Rookie of the Year. Shaquille is known by many different names for example, Shaq, Diesel, The Big Aristotle, Superman, Shaq Fu, etc. The teams that he played for are the Orlando Magic, Los Angeles Lakers, Miami Heat, Phoenix Suns, Cleveland Cavaliers, and Boston Celtics.
